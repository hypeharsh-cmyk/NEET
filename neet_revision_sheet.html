Top 20 Must-Remember Scientists

  1. Robert Hooke - discovered the cell, 1665.
  2. Antonie van Leeuwenhoek - first observed and described a live cell.
  3. Robert Brown - discovered nucleus, 1831.
  4. Schleiden and Schwann - cell theory for plants and animals, 1838-1839.
  5. Rudolf Virchow - omnis cellula e cellula, 1855.
  6. Gregor Mendel - laws of inheritance, 1865.
  7. Sutton and Boveri - chromosomal theory of inheritance, 1902.
  8. Thomas Hunt Morgan - experimental proof of chromosomal theory, 1910.
  9. Watson and Crick - DNA double helix model, 1953.
  10. Frederick Griffith - transformation principle, 1928.
  11. Avery, MacLeod and McCarty - DNA as genetic material, 1944.
  12. Hershey and Chase - confirmed DNA as genetic material, 1952.
  13. Meselson and Stahl - semi-conservative DNA replication, 1958.
  14. Charles Darwin - natural selection, 1859.
  15. Alexander Fleming - penicillin discovery, 1928.
  16. Edward Jenner - smallpox vaccine, 1796.
  17. Joseph Priestley - role of air in plant growth, 1770.
  18. Melvin Calvin - C3 cycle / Calvin cycle.
  19. Hans Krebs - Krebs cycle.
